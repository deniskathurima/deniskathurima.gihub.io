ABOUT CHYMA DENIS INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY

VISION

To be an International Centre of excellence in I.C.T

MISSION

To provide TIVET for producing Dependable skilled human resource for commerce and industry .

History of CHYMA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY.

CHYMA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY was founded way back in 2015. It was started to increase the technology so as to reach the vision 2030. The management so that it's good to start a few programs of Information Communication Technology. Among them are MIT Android and Web development. The instituste has got also Diploma in Information Communication Technology.

ACCOMODATION

The institution has got 5 hostels for men and 5 for ladies which are allocated on first come first serve basis.Students are allowed to select the rooms they wish to stay in.

Counseling

CHYMA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY has a very active guidance and counseling department whose objective is to assist students and the entire INSTITUTE community in developing self knowledge, emotional acceptance and growth in all aspects of human development. The department seeks to address and resolve students problems as well as assisting them cope in times of crisis so that they can remain focused on their academic pursuits.

SPORTS and RECREATION

CHYMA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY has got an active sports and games department whose main aim is to make INSTITUTE a competitive center of excellence in sports training and recreation.The department main aim is to offer recreational and leisure opportunity for students and staff and also to avail an opportunity for students to compete in local,national and international championships . Students Union CHYMA INSTITUTE OF TECHNOLOGY has got an active student congress headed by the prime minister who is elected by the students themselves. Co-curriculum activities in 1st Term 2015.
